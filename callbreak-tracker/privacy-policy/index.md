## 1. Information I Collect

### 1.1 No Personal Data Collection

Callbreak Tracker does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.  
The App does **not** require login or account creation.  
Everything you enter in the App is stored **locally on your device only**.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** for crash reporting.  
Crashlytics automatically collects:

- Device model
- OS version
- Crash logs
- App version
- Basic diagnostic data

This data is **anonymous** and used only to fix crashes and improve app stability.

No gameplay data, names, scores, or personal identifiers are collected.
