1. Information I Collect

1.1 No Personal Data Collection

Callbreak Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.
The App does not require login or account creation.
All user-entered data is stored locally on your device only.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting.
Crashlytics automatically collects anonymous diagnostic data such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Crash logs
  • App version
  • Basic technical data

No gameplay data, names, scores, or personally identifying information are collected.

2. How the Information Is Used

Crashlytics data is used only for:

  • Identifying crashes
  • Improving stability
  • Enhancing app performance

No information is used for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses:

Firebase Crashlytics

A crash reporting service provided by Google.
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The App does not use ads, trackers, analytics, or any other third-party SDKs.

4. Data Storage

  • All gameplay data remains on your device.
  • Nothing is stored on external servers.
  • Uninstalling the App permanently removes all stored data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, the App complies with standard child-protection regulations (COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar).

6. Your Rights

Because no personal data is stored:

  • I cannot access, view, or retrieve your data.
  • You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App.
